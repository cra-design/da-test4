TD1 form, part 1 – Video

Congratulations on starting your first job or getting a new one! When starting, your employer probably gave you a form called the Personal Tax Credits Return or TD1 form. It’s super important to fill out your TD1 form correctly. Don’t worry, we’ll help you through the process!

TD1 form, part 2 – Video

In this video, we’ll guide you through the process of filling out the form correctly, which is important for your taxes. The first page has sections for tax credits that apply to you, like the basic personal amount and tuition. On the second page, there are additional sections based on your personal situation. Don’t forget to sign and date the form and give it to your employer. This is the second part of our TD1 series, so make sure to watch the first part if you haven’t already.
